5-step root cause walkthrough for top exception — MIS-52020 · Mischips Magic Masala 52g · MUM · IgnoredDemand = -42,000
Sourcing pattern: Pattern A — Mumbai sourced directly from Mischips Foods Pvt Ltd. Lead time = plant dispatch (2d) + transleadtime (5d) = 7 days.

STEP 1
Is demand itself abnormal?
Demand flat at historical avg ~1,450 units/day. No forecast bias signal. No outlier — demand is normal.
→ Not the root cause. Proceed to Step 2.
STEP 2
Does exception fall inside the lead time window?
Exception start: 02 Apr. Lead time horizon: today (02 Apr) + 7d = 09 Apr.
Exceptions run 02 Apr – 30 Apr. First bucket (02 Apr – 09 Apr) IS inside lead time — too late to order.
Remaining window (10 Apr – 30 Apr) is outside lead time. Supply could have been planned. No SchedRcpts, no TotInTransitIn for either window.
→ Partial timing cause for first 7 days. Structural gap for remaining 21 days. Proceed to Step 3.
STEP 3 ✦
Is there any supply planned or in motion?
TotInTransitIn = 0 for the full 30-day window. SchedRcpts = 0.
Engine generated no recommendation and no order is in motion.
Signal: TotInTransitIn = 0 outside lead time horizon.
Likely cause: Mischips allocation flag suppressed engine recommendations. The Q2 allocation cut means the engine could not project receivable supply → no order generated.
STEP 4
Is an alternate source available? (DynDepSrc)
DynDepSrc: not configured for MIS-52020 at Mumbai. No alternate hub or backup vendor lane in system.
→ No alternate sourcing possible. Confirms Step 3 finding — single source, no fallback.
STEP 5
Is shelf life the constraint?
ConstrProjOH = -42,000. OH = 0 (no physical stock). This is a volume gap, not shelf-life exclusion.
→ Not shelf-life related. Root cause is Step 3.
Root Cause — Step 3: No supply planned (TotInTransitIn = 0)
Engine generated no replenishment recommendation for MIS-52020 at Mumbai across the full 30-day review window. Primary driver: Mischips Foods vendor allocation cut (Q2) suppressed planning engine recommendations. No alternate source (DynDepSrc not configured). First 7 days are also inside lead time horizon — timing root cause. The remaining 21-day gap is structural.
